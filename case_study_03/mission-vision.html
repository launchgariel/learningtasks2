Mission and Vision

Our Mission

University of LANZ'S is committed to providing quality education grounded in Lasallian values. We strive to:

  • Nurture academic excellence and intellectual growth in every student
  • Foster spiritual development and moral integrity
  • Prepare students for meaningful careers and responsible citizenship
  • Promote social awareness and commitment to serve others
  • Cultivate creativity, critical thinking, and innovation

Our Vision

University of LANZ'S aspires to be:

  • A leading Catholic educational institution in the region
  • A center of excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement
  • A community where faith, culture, and education converge
  • A catalyst for positive social transformation
  • An institution that produces graduates who are competent, compassionate, and committed to the common good

Our Core Values

Faith: We believe in God and nurture spiritual growth in our community.

Zeal: We serve with passion, dedication, and commitment to excellence.

Communion: We build community through collaboration, respect, and mutual support.
